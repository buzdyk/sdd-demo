# Build the landing page

## Problem

MVP scope is "a domain pointing at the blog, single page for now." That single page is the entire shippable surface area — it has to introduce who the author is and what the blog is, even with no posts behind it yet.

## Approach

- Decide what minimum content the page needs: name, one-line bio, what to expect, contact link.
- Implement once the static generator is chosen ([[CHOOSE_STATIC_GENERATOR]]).
- Keep styling minimal and inline — a full theme system is post-MVP ([[DESIGN_BASE_THEME]]).
